Image Format Optimization

What format is the most optimal for this type of image and why?

With what compression quality does the image not lose quality?

Can we reduce its size even more without losing quality?

What would happen if we save the image several times with JPEG format?

JPEG would be the optimal format, since the image has lots of different colours and gradients. And until around ~70% compression strength, there isn't a very large noticeable difference in quality loss. But more than that, the quality will rapidly degrade and it will be very visible. Also, if we repeatedly convert the image to the same format, we would notice a loss in quality, albeit, you'll need to convert and reconvert a lot of times to actually notice much.
